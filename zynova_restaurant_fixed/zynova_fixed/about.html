Cooking the way it used to take.

Z Y N O V A was built for people who miss the taste of food that wasn't rushed — ground by hand, cooked over real fire, and served without pretence.

Our mission

Honest ingredients, unhurried cooking, fair prices.

We exist to prove that a proper meal doesn't need shortcuts to be quick to enjoy — only patient hands behind the scenes. Every masala is ground fresh each morning, every curry base simmers for hours, and every price on our menu reflects exactly what's in the dish, nothing hidden.

We work directly with three farming families outside Chennai for our vegetables and lentils, and we butcher and portion our own meat daily rather than holding pre-cut stock.

In one sentence

"Cook slow, price fair, waste nothing."

That's the line painted above our kitchen pass, and every dish that leaves it is measured against it.

Our story

How Zynova came to be

2019 — Besant Nagar, Chennai

Zynova opens with one charcoal tandoor, a stone grinder and a five-dish menu.

2021 — The Chettinad line

We add a dedicated Tamil Nadu regional menu — Chettinad mutton, kothu parotta, meen kuzhambu.

2023 — 4.8★ across 2,000+ reviews

Word of mouth carries us past our 500th regular guest family.

Today

Same fire, same grinding stone, a longer menu — and this website, finally redesigned properly.

What we do

Three things, done properly

Open-fire cooking

A working charcoal tandoor and grill sit at the centre of our kitchen — most mains pass through it.

Daily-ground spice

No pre-mixed masala jars. Whole spices are toasted and stone-ground fresh each morning.

Scratch-made breads

Naan, kulcha, parotta and roti are proofed, shaped and baked to order — never held under a lamp.
