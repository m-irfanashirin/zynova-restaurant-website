Welcome

A kitchen built around one fire and one grinding stone.

Z Y N O V A opened with a simple rule: nothing goes out that wasn't started from raw ingredients that morning. That means whole spices toasted and ground daily, bread proofed in-house, and every curry base simmered for hours before service even begins.

Straight from Tamil Nadu

Chettinad & coastal specials, made properly

Alongside our tandoori classics, we run a full Tamil Nadu regional line — dry-roasted Chettinad mutton, Kerala-style fish curry, filter coffee and more. Ask for it "naatu style" and we'll turn up the heat.
